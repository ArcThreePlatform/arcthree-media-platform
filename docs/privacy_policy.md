ArcThree Platform ("we", "our", or "the Platform") respects user privacy and is committed to protecting personal information.

This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and managed when using ArcThree Platform and related services.

- KPI and analytics data

Collected information may be used for:

- Social media publishing management
- KPI analysis
- Content workflow management
- Service improvement

## 4. TikTok API Usage

ArcThree Platform may use TikTok APIs, including Content Posting API, for:

- Creator-approved content publishing
- Publishing workflow management
- KPI tracking and analytics

We do not use TikTok APIs for:

- Spam activity
- Unauthorized posting
- Mass account farming
- Automated abusive behavior
